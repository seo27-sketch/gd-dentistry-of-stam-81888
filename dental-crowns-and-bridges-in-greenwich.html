Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Crowns and Bridges

How much do dental crowns and bridges typically cost at GD Dentistry of Stamford in Greenwich, CT?
The cost for dental crowns at GD Dentistry of Stamford generally ranges from $1,300 to $1,650 per tooth, while dental bridges are typically priced between $3,500 and $5,500, depending on the number of teeth involved and materials used. We provide detailed treatment plans and review your insurance coverage before starting any work. Our Greenwich patients find that we’re transparent about all costs up front, and we offer payment options to help manage out-of-pocket expenses.
How long does the process of getting a crown or bridge take from start to finish?
For most patients at our Greenwich office, getting a crown or bridge takes two to three visits over two to three weeks. The first appointment involves preparing the tooth, taking digital impressions, and placing a temporary crown or bridge. Your custom restoration is then fabricated at a local dental lab. Once it's ready—usually within 10 to 14 days—you’ll come back for a fitting and permanent placement. We also offer expedited options for certain cases.
Is GD Dentistry of Stamford properly licensed, and are your dentists experienced in restorative procedures like crowns and bridges?
Absolutely. All our dentists are fully licensed by the Connecticut State Dental Commission and regularly complete continuing education in restorative dentistry. Dr. Green and Dr. Daniels have over 25 years of combined experience placing crowns and bridges, with hundreds of successful cases for patients throughout Greenwich and Fairfield County.
What is the process for getting a dental crown or bridge at your Greenwich office?
The process begins with a thorough exam and digital X-rays to determine the best solution. We’ll discuss your options and answer all your questions. For crowns and bridges, we carefully numb the area, shape the tooth (or teeth), and take precise digital impressions. You’ll get a temporary restoration while the permanent one is crafted. At your follow-up visit, we ensure a perfect fit, make any necessary adjustments, and bond the crown or bridge securely. Throughout, we focus on comfort and clear communication.
Do crowns and bridges from GD Dentistry of Stamford come with any guarantees or warranties?
Yes, we stand behind our dental work. All crowns and bridges placed at GD Dentistry of Stamford are covered by a five-year warranty against defects in materials or workmanship, provided you maintain recommended checkups and hygiene visits. If any issues arise within that period, please contact our Greenwich office and we’ll address them promptly.
