Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Implants

How much do dental implants typically cost at GD Dentistry of Stamford near Norwalk, CT?
At GD Dentistry of Stamford, the cost for a single dental implant—including the implant, abutment, and crown—generally ranges from $4,000 to $5,500. The final price depends on factors like the need for bone grafting, the type of implant used, and whether additional procedures are required. We offer complimentary consultations to provide a personalized estimate and discuss financing options to help make treatment more affordable for Norwalk-area patients.
How long does it take to get a dental implant from start to finish at your practice?
The dental implant process at GD Dentistry of Stamford typically takes between 3 to 6 months from initial consultation to final crown placement. After your consultation and 3D imaging, we’ll schedule the implant placement. Healing and integration with the jawbone (osseointegration) usually takes about 3–4 months. Once healed, we place the abutment and custom crown. Some cases, especially those needing bone grafting, may take a bit longer. We’ll outline your personalized timeline during your first visit.
Are your dentists licensed and experienced in placing dental implants?
Yes, all our dentists are fully licensed in Connecticut and have advanced training in implant dentistry. Our team regularly participates in continuing education and uses the latest technology, including 3D imaging, to ensure safe and successful implant procedures for our Norwalk-area patients.
What is the process for getting a dental implant at GD Dentistry of Stamford?
The process begins with a comprehensive exam, including 3D imaging, to assess your bone health and plan your implant. If you’re a good candidate, we’ll schedule the implant surgery in our office under local anesthesia. After the implant is placed, you’ll have a healing period of several months. Once the implant is integrated, we add an abutment and take impressions for your permanent crown. We work closely with you at each step to ensure comfort and a natural-looking result.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties on dental implants?
We stand behind the quality of our dental implants. GD Dentistry of Stamford provides a 5-year warranty on implant crowns and abutments, provided patients attend regular checkups and maintain good oral hygiene. If you experience any issues, our team is here to address them promptly. The titanium implant itself is designed to last many years with proper care, and we’ll review all warranty details with you during your consultation.
